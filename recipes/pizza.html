Pizza

Recipe

Pizza is a popular dish that originated in Italy, made from a flat, round dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of other ingredients like vegetables, meats, or herbs. It’s baked in an oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Pizza comes in many styles—thin crust, thick crust, stuffed crust, and even deep-dish—and is loved worldwide for its combination of flavors and versatility.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • water
  • salt
  • sugar
  • yeast
  • olive oil
  • tomato
  • garlic
  • basil
  • oregano
  • mozarella cheese
  • olives
  • pepperoni

Step for making the pizza

  1. Make the dough: Mix flour, yeast, water, salt, sugar, and oil → knead → let rise.
  2. prepare the sauce: Cook tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, salt, and herbs.
  3. Assemble pizza: Roll out dough, spread sauce, add cheese and toppings.
  4. Bake: Cook in a preheated oven at 220°C (430°F) for 12–15 minutes until crust is golden.
  5. Serve: Slice and enjoy hot!

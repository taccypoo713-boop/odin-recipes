Description

Omelette is a simple and versatile dish made from beaten eggs cooked in a pan, often folded around a filling. It can be plain or include ingredients like cheese, vegetables (onions, tomatoes, bell peppers), herbs, or meats (ham, bacon). Omelettes are quick to make, usually ready in just a few minutes, and can be served for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. They are soft, fluffy, and savory, making them a favorite comfort food worldwide.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • oil
  • cilantro

Steps for making omlette

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl, add salt and pepper, and whisk well.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and let them cook for a few seconds.
  4. Gently fold the omelette and cook until fully set.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
  6. Serve hot.
